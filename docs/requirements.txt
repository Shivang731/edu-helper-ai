
## UI/UX
- **Gradient header** with fixed top menu for About & Connect  
- **Dark modern theme** with custom fonts and box shadows  
- **Responsive layout**: sidebar controls & main content panels

## Data Flow
1. User uploads doc  
2. Text extraction & cleaning  
3. AI summarization  
4. Flashcard & audio generation  
5. Semantic search indexing  
6. Optional export to Markdown
